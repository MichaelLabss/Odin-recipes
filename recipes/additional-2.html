Below is a full list of the purposes for every ingredient in this classic waffle recipe:

  • Eggs - Gives waffles their airy texture
  • All-purpose flour - All-purpose flour adds gluten, which gives the waffles structure. You can also use whole wheat flour.
  • Milk - Whole milk adds richness and moisture. Plus, it helps create the batter consistency.
  • Vegetable oil - A neutral oil, such as vegetable oil, ensures the waffles are nice and moist.
  • White sugar - A tablespoon of white sugar adds the perfect amount of sweetness
  • Baking powder - Baking powder acts as a leavener (a substance that releases gas when mixed with other ingredients, causing the batter to rise), which means it contributes to the waffles' light and fluffy texture
  • Salt - A pinch of salt goes a long way. It balances out the sugar's sweetness, and enhances other ingredients' flavors too! Don't skip this step!
  • Vanilla extract - Vanilla extract helps enhance other ingedients flavors but, you only need a dash!
  • Nonstick cooking spray - This helps the waffles not stick to the iron you are using.
