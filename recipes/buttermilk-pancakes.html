Buttermilk Pancakes

Home

Description:

These Buttermilk Pancakes are Light, fluffy, moist, and full of rich flavor! And their easy to make; store bought mixes may be convenient, but every home cook needs a good homemade buttermilk pancake recipe.

Ingredients

Original recipe (1X) yields 12 servings

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder, 1 ½ teapoons baking soda; Leavners - Baking powder and baking soda act as leaveners (a substance that releases gas when mixed with other ingredients, causing the batter to rise)
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • ½ Milk
  • 3 largs eggs
  • ⅓ cup butter,melted
