Below is a full list of the puproses for every ingredient in this buttermilk pancake recipe:

  • Flour - All-purpose flour contains gluten, which gives the buttermilk pancakes structure and chewiness.
  • Sugar - 3 tablespoons of white sugar adds the right ammount of sweetness to the pancakes.
  • Leavners - Baking powder and baking soda act as leaveners (a substance that releases gas when mixed with other ingredients, causing the batter to rise.
  • Salt - Enhances flavor and balances out sweetness; pancakes will taste bland or flat without it.
  • Buttermilk - Buttermilk is needed for buttermilk pancakes ( you will need 3 cups!)
  • Milk - Wholemilk helps create the perfect batter consistency and cuts some of the buttermilk's acidity.
  • Eggs - 3 whole eggs (egg white and yolk) will add the perfect ammount of flavor and structure.
  • Butter - Keeps pancakes nice and moist.
